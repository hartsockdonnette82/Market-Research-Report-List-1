<p><p>The Portable Industrial Emissions Analyzer market comprises two main types: Gas Pollutant Analyzers and Particle Pollutant Analyzers. Gas Pollutant Analyzers detect and measure harmful gases such as carbon monoxide, nitrogen oxides, and volatile organic compounds emitted from industrial processes. In contrast, Particle Pollutant Analyzers quantify airborne particulate matter, assessing pollutants like dust and smoke that can impact air quality and human health. Both analyzer types play crucial roles in environmental monitoring and compliance with emissions regulations.</p></p>
