<p><p>The electromagnetic clutch system market is categorized into single disk and multi-disk clutches. Single disk clutches typically feature one friction surface, making them simpler and cost-effective, suitable for lighter applications. In contrast, multi-disk clutches consist of multiple friction surfaces, allowing for higher torque capacity and more efficient power transmission, ideal for heavy-duty applications. Both types leverage electromagnetic force for engagement and disengagement, providing precise control, minimal wear, and enhanced operational efficiency in various industrial and automotive applications.</p></p>
