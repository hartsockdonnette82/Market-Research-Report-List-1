<p><strong>The Anti-Infective Vaccines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inactivated Vaccines</li><li>Conjugate Vaccines</li><li>Live/attenuated Vaccines</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The anti-infective vaccines market comprises various types, including inactivated, conjugate, and live/attenuated vaccines. Inactivated vaccines contain killed pathogens, inducing immunity without the risk of disease, ideal for viruses like influenza. Conjugate vaccines link polysaccharides from bacterial capsules to proteins, enhancing immune response, especially in young children, as seen with pneumococcal vaccines. Live/attenuated vaccines use weakened pathogens, providing robust, long-lasting immunity, effective against diseases like measles and mumps. Each type plays a critical role in preventing infectious diseases.</p></p>
