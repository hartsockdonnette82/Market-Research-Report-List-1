<p><strong>The ID Card& Credit Card Printers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dye Sub Printers</li><li>Inkjet Printers</li><li>Retransfer Printers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ID card and credit card printer market features three main types: dye sublimation printers, inkjet printers, and retransfer printers. Dye sublimation printers use heat to transfer dye onto the card, producing high-quality, durable images ideal for ID cards. Inkjet printers employ liquid ink, offering versatility and lower initial costs but may lack durability for frequent handling. Retransfer printers print onto a film that is then fused to the card, providing superior image quality and durability, making them suitable for secure applications.</p></p>
