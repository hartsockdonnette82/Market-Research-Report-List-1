<p><strong>The Brake Tester Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Roller Brake Testers</li><li>Plate Brake Testers</li><li>Decelerometers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The brake tester market comprises three primary types: roller brake testers, plate brake testers, and decelerometers. Roller brake testers use rollers to simulate road conditions, measuring braking force and efficiency for vehicles. Plate brake testers involve a flat platform on which a vehicle is driven, assessing brake performance through various tests. Decelerometers, on the other hand, measure the vehicle's deceleration during braking, providing data on brake effectiveness. Each type plays a crucial role in vehicle safety and maintenance assessments.</p></p>
