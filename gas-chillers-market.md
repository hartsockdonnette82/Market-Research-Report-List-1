<p><ul><li>Open Loop</li><li>Closed Loop</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The gas chillers market is divided into open loop and closed loop systems. Open loop gas chillers utilize ambient air or water as a heat sink, often found in locations with substantial water sources or where environmental conditions favor efficiency. In contrast, closed loop gas chillers circulate a refrigerant through a sealed system, enhancing energy efficiency and minimizing environmental impact. Both types cater to diverse industrial needs, providing effective cooling solutions while emphasizing sustainability and operational efficiency.</p></p>
