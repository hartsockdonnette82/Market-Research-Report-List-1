<p><ul><li>J Tip</li><li>Straight Tip</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PTFE guide wire market is primarily categorized into two types: J Tip and Straight Tip guide wires. J Tip guide wires feature a curved end, allowing for easier navigation through complex anatomical pathways, making them suitable for challenging vascular procedures. In contrast, Straight Tip guide wires have a linear design, providing direct access and stability in less convoluted channels. Both types serve essential roles in medical interventions, facilitating the placement of catheter systems in various diagnostic and therapeutic applications.</p></p>
