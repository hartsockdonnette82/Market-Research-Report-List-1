<p><p>The Ethylene Propylene Diene Monomer (EPDM) rubber market is primarily categorized into two types based on the polymerization method: solution polymerization and suspension polymerization. </p><p>Solution polymerization involves dissolving monomers in a solvent, allowing for better control over the molecular weight and polymer structure, resulting in high-quality rubber with superior properties. </p><p>In contrast, suspension polymerization occurs in an aqueous medium, leading to a different structure and properties, typically yielding a higher-viscosity rubber suitable for specific applications like automotive and industrial uses.</p></p>
