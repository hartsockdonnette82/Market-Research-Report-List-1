<p><p>The Polymer Resettable Fuse market is categorized into two main types: In-Line and Surface Mount. In-Line fuses are designed to be integrated into the circuit path, providing protection by interrupting the current during overload conditions. Surface Mount fuses, on the other hand, are compact and can be soldered directly onto a circuit board, enabling space-efficient designs. Both types offer resettable capabilities, allowing them to restore functionality once the fault condition is cleared, making them ideal for modern electronic applications.</p></p>
