<p><p>Hospital asset management encompasses various technologies that enhance the tracking and management of equipment and inventory. Real-time Location Systems (RTLS) utilize a network of fixed and mobile sensors to provide precise location data for assets. Radio-frequency Identification (RFID) employs tags and readers to monitor equipment status and movements. Ultrasound systems use sound waves for tracking assets in real-time within a facility, while Infrared technology utilizes light signals to detect and manage equipment locations and movements, improving operational efficiency.</p></p>
