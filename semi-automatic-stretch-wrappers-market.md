<p><p>Semi-automatic stretch wrappers are packaging machines that partially automate the process of wrapping palletized loads with stretch film, requiring some manual intervention. The market includes three main types: **Rotary Stretch Wrappers**, where the load rotates on a turntable while the film is applied; **Turntable Stretch Wrappers**, which rotate the load while the film carriage remains stationary; and **Robotic Stretch Wrappers**, which use robotic arms to wrap complex or irregularly shaped loads. Each type caters to specific packaging needs, balancing efficiency, precision, and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
