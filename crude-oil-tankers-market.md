<p><p>The crude oil tanker market comprises several vessel types categorized by deadweight tonnage (DWT). Panamax vessels, ranging from 60,000 to 80,000 DWT, can navigate through the Panama Canal. Aframax tankers, with 80,000 to 120,000 DWT, are suited for shorter hauls and can access various ports. Suezmax vessels, between 120,000 and 200,000 DWT, are designed to transit the Suez Canal. VLCCs, ranging from 200,000 to 300,000 DWT, and ULCCs, exceeding 300,000 DWT, are used for long-haul transportation of crude oil.</p></p>
