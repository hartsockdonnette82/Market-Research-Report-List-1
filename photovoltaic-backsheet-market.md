<p><p>The photovoltaic backsheet market is segmented into fluoropolymer and non-fluoropolymer types. Fluoropolymer backsheets are known for their excellent durability, weather resistance, and UV protection, making them suitable for high-performance solar panels. In contrast, non-fluoropolymer backsheets, often made from materials like polyester or polyamide, offer a cost-effective solution but may have shorter lifespans and lower resistance to environmental stress. Both types serve to protect the solar cells while influencing performance, longevity, and overall system efficiency.</p></p>
