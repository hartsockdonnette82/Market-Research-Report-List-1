<p><p>The Angle Seat Valves market can be categorized into three main types based on their end connections: Flanged End, Threaded End, and Others. Flanged End valves feature flat surfaces that are bolted together, providing a secure and leak-proof seal, ideal for high-pressure applications. Threaded End valves are connected with threads, allowing for ease of installation and removal in smaller systems. The 'Others' category includes specialized connections like socket weld or clamp ends, catering to unique industrial requirements.</p></p>
