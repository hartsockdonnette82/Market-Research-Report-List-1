<p><p>The polyester resins market is primarily segmented into unsaturated and saturated polyester resins. Unsaturated polyester resins (UPRs) are widely used in applications like fiber-reinforced plastics and automotive parts due to their excellent mechanical properties and corrosion resistance. Conversely, saturated polyester resins (SPRs) are employed in coatings, adhesives, and textiles, offering enhanced durability and weather resistance. Both types serve diverse industries, with unsaturated resins focusing on structural applications, while saturated resins excel in protective and aesthetic applications.</p></p>
