<p><p>A Rail Mounted Gantry (RMG) Crane is a specialized lifting equipment designed for container handling operations at ports and intermodal yards. RMG cranes are essential for enhancing operational efficiency, reducing turnaround times, and optimizing space utilization in container terminals.</p><p>The market for RMG cranes is witnessing significant growth, driven by the increasing demand for containerized cargo transportation and the expansion of port facilities worldwide.
