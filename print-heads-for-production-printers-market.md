<p><strong>The Print Heads for Production Printers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Piezoelectric Print Heads</li><li>Thermal Print Heads</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Print heads are critical components in production printers, with two main types: piezoelectric and thermal. Piezoelectric print heads utilize electrical charges to deform crystals, controlling ink ejection with high precision and allowing for a wider range of ink types. In contrast, thermal print heads heat ink to create vapor bubbles that force it out through nozzles, making them ideal for dye-based inks. Both technologies cater to different printing needs, influencing print quality, speed, and application suitability.</p></p>
