<p><ul><li>Dye Sub</li><li>Retransfer</li><li>Inkjet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Card personalization solutions encompass various printing technologies, including dye sublimation, retransfer, and inkjet. Dye sublimation involves transferring dye onto the card surface, ensuring vibrant colors and high durability, ideal for high-volume applications. Retransfer printing offers superior image quality by printing onto a film before fusing it to the card, providing enhanced security and durability. Inkjet printing, while typically used for lower volume needs, offers flexibility and cost-effectiveness, making it suitable for customizable designs and smaller batch production in card personalization.</p></p>
