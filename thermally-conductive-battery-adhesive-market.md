<p><strong>The Thermally Conductive Battery Adhesive Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gap Fillers</li><li>Structural Adhesives</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thermally conductive battery adhesive market includes two primary types: gap fillers and structural adhesives. Gap fillers are designed to eliminate air gaps between surfaces, enhancing thermal management and ensuring efficient heat dissipation in battery packs. Structural adhesives, on the other hand, provide robust bonding while maintaining thermal conductivity, essential for high-performance applications in electric vehicles and consumer electronics. Together, they optimize battery performance, reliability, and lifespan by effectively managing heat flow and mechanical integrity.</p></p>
