<p><p>The midrange speaker market encompasses three primary types. Cone types feature a cone-shaped diaphragm, delivering smooth and accurate sound reproduction suitable for various applications. Dome types use a dome-shaped diaphragm that offers wide dispersion and clarity, often found in home audio systems. Compression horn types utilize a diaphragm driven by a compression driver, amplifying sound through a horn for higher efficiency. Each type caters to different consumer needs, from home theater setups to professional audio environments, ensuring optimal midrange performance.</p></p>
