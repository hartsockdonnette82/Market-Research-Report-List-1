<p><p>The passenger vehicle tire molds market is primarily divided into segmented molds and two-piece molds. Segmented molds consist of multiple sections that can be interchanged, allowing for flexibility in tire design and production efficiency. In contrast, two-piece molds are simpler, comprising an upper and lower part that come together, typically used for standard tire shapes. Both types cater to varying production needs, with segmented molds offering customization options, while two-piece molds provide straightforward manufacturing processes.</p></p>
