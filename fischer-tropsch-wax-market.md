<p><p>The Fischer-Tropsch wax market is segmented by carbon chain length into C35-C80, C80-C100, and C100+ types. C80-C100 waxes have higher melting points, making them ideal for applications requiring durability, such as candles and rubber processing. C100+ waxes, with the longest carbon chains, are used in specialized applications like lubricants and high-performance coatings, offering superior thermal stability and resistance to wear. Each type caters to specific industrial needs based on their unique properties.</p></p>
