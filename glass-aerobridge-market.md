<p><p>The Glass Aerobridge market features two main elevation systems: the Electromechanical Elevation System and the Hydraulic Elevation System. The Electromechanical system uses electric motors and gears to facilitate smooth and precise movement of the bridge, ensuring reliable operation. In contrast, the Hydraulic Elevation System relies on hydraulic fluid to generate powerful lifting capabilities, often providing a robust solution for heavy-duty applications. Both systems enhance passenger boarding experiences while offering distinct advantages in terms of maintenance and operational efficiency.</p></p>
