<p><p>Thyristor Surge Protection Devices (TSPD) are designed to safeguard electrical systems from voltage surges and transients. The market for TSPDs is segmented into two main types: Bidirectional TSPDs can handle surges in both directions, making them suitable for AC applications. Unidirectional TSPDs, on the other hand, are designed for controlling surges in one direction, primarily used in DC applications. Each type serves specific needs within surge protection systems.</p></p>
