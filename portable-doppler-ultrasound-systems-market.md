<p><p>The Portable Doppler Ultrasound Systems market is categorized primarily into Directional Doppler and Non-Directional Doppler types. Directional Doppler systems provide information about the flow direction of blood, making them useful for specific diagnostic applications, particularly in cardiology. In contrast, Non-Directional Doppler systems measure the overall velocity of blood flow without indicating its direction, making them simpler and often more compact. Both types cater to different clinical needs, enhancing diagnostics in various healthcare settings.</p></p>
