<p><strong>The Consumer Grade GS CMOS Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Front-Illuminated Structure</li><li>Back-Illuminated Structure</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The consumer-grade GS CMOS sensor market is primarily divided into two types: front-illuminated and back-illuminated structures. Front-illuminated sensors capture light through the sensor's circuitry, offering lower production costs but typically leading to reduced sensitivity and dynamic range. In contrast, back-illuminated sensors have the circuitry behind the light-sensitive area, enhancing light collection, improving low-light performance, and overall image quality. This makes back-illuminated structures increasingly popular in smartphones and cameras, driving their adoption in the consumer market.</p></p>
