<p><strong>The Glass Cutting Oil Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mineral Oil</li><li>Water Soluble Oil</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The glass cutting oil market primarily consists of two types: mineral oil and water-soluble oil. Mineral oil is derived from petroleum and offers excellent lubrication and cooling properties, making it ideal for heavy-duty cutting operations. Water-soluble oils, on the other hand, are emulsifiable with water, providing easier clean-up and safer handling while reducing smoke and odors during cutting. Both types cater to different industrial needs, ensuring optimal performance in glass processing applications while enhancing tool life and product quality.</p></p>
