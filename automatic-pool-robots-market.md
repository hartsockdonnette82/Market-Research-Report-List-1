<p><strong>The Automatic Pool Robots Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Crawler Drive</li><li>Wheel Drive</li><li>Self-propelled (Floating)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automatic Pool Robots market features three primary types of drive systems: Crawler Drive, Wheel Drive, and Self-Propelled (Floating). Crawler Drive robots use tracks for enhanced maneuverability on various surfaces, making them ideal for larger or irregularly shaped pools. Wheel Drive robots employ wheels to navigate efficiently, typically offering faster cleaning speeds. Self-Propelled (Floating) models float on the water's surface, utilizing water propulsion for movement, allowing for effective cleaning without needing a physical connection to the pool's edge.</p></p>
