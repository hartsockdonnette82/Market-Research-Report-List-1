<p><p>The Solid Phase Extraction (SPE) consumables market includes three primary types: cartridges, well plates, and disks. Cartridges are cylindrical containers filled with sorbent materials that facilitate the extraction of analytes from liquid samples. Well plates, designed for high-throughput applications, allow multiple samples to be processed simultaneously, enhancing efficiency in laboratories. Disks, often used for large-volume or specific analyte extraction, provide a flat surface area for improved separation. Each type serves distinct needs within analytical chemistry and environmental testing.</p></p>
