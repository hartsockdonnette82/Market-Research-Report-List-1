<p><ul><li>Viscose Filament Yarn</li><li>Viscose Staple Fiber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The viscose yarn market comprises two main types: Viscose Filament Yarn (VFY) and Viscose Staple Fiber (VSF). VFY is a continuous, smooth yarn used in textiles like sarees, linings, and lightweight fabrics due to its silk-like appearance and durability. VSF, on the other hand, consists of short fibers spun into yarns, widely used in apparel, home textiles, and non-woven products for its softness and versatility. Both types are derived from cellulose, offering eco-friendly, biodegradable alternatives to synthetic fibers.</p></p>
