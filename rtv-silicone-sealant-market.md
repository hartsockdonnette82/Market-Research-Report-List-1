<p><ul><li>Single Component Silicone Sealant</li><li>Double Component Silicone Sealant</li></ul></p>
<p><p>RTV (Room Temperature Vulcanizing) silicone sealants are categorized into two main types: single component and double component. Single component silicone sealants cure upon exposure to moisture in the air, making them user-friendly and ideal for various applications. In contrast, double component silicone sealants consist of a base and a curing agent that must be mixed before application, resulting in a stronger bond and greater durability. Each type serves distinct needs in construction, automotive, and industrial sectors.</p></p>
