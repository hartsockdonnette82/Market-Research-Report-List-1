<p><p>The liquid macrofiltration filter market encompasses various filter types designed for separating solids from liquids. Granular micro filters utilize granules for effective filtration, while filter press filters apply high pressure to achieve solid-liquid separation. Leaf tubular and press filters use flat or tubular membranes for efficient processing. Bag filters capture particulates in removable bags, and drum and disk filters utilize rotating components. Belt filter presses combine mechanical pressure for dewatering, while AWB filters are advanced water-based systems. Other filters may include specialized technologies for niche applications.</p></p>
