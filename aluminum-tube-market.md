<p><ul><li>Seamless Aluminum Tube</li><li>Welded Aluminum Tube</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aluminum tube market is divided into two main types: seamless and welded aluminum tubes. Seamless aluminum tubes are manufactured without seams, offering superior strength and resistance to pressure, making them ideal for applications in aerospace, automotive, and high-pressure environments. In contrast, welded aluminum tubes are produced by joining two edges of aluminum sheets, allowing for a wide variety of shapes and sizes. They are commonly used in construction, furniture, and plumbing, where cost efficiency and versatility are key.</p></p>
