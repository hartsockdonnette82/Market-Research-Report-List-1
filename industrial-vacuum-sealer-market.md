<p><p>The industrial vacuum sealer market is primarily divided into two types: nozzle vacuum sealers and chamber vacuum sealers. Nozzle vacuum sealers utilize a nozzle to remove air from bags, making them ideal for sealing larger items or irregular shapes. In contrast, chamber vacuum sealers operate within a closed chamber, creating a vacuum around the product, which allows for more effective sealing and better preservation. Both types cater to various industries, including food packaging, pharmaceuticals, and manufacturing, enhancing product shelf life and quality.</p></p>
