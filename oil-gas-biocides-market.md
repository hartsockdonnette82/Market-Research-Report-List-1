<p><ul><li>Oxidizing Fungicide</li><li>Non-Oxidizing Fungicide</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Oil & Gas Biocides market is divided into two primary types: oxidizing and non-oxidizing fungicides. Oxidizing fungicides act by damaging the cellular structure of microorganisms, effectively controlling biofouling and microbial growth in oil and gas systems. Non-oxidizing fungicides, on the other hand, inhibit microbial reproduction and can provide prolonged protection against bacterial and fungal infestations. Both types play a critical role in maintaining operational efficiency, safety, and regulatory compliance in the oil and gas industry.</p></p>
