<p><strong>The Biogas Mixers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Submersible Mixers</li><li>Dry-installed Mixers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The biogas mixers market comprises primarily two types: submersible mixers and dry-installed mixers. Submersible mixers operate underwater, directly agitating the biomass in anaerobic digesters to enhance mixing and gas production efficiency. They are effective in maintaining homogeneous conditions and optimizing microbial activity. In contrast, dry-installed mixers are positioned above the digestion tank and use a mechanical system to circulate the substrate. They are easier to maintain and install but may have limited mixing capabilities compared to submersible options.</p></p>
