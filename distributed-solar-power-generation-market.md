<p><p>Distributed solar power generation is primarily categorized into rooftop and ground-mounted systems. Rooftop solar systems are installed on residential, commercial, or industrial buildings, maximizing space utilization and providing energy directly where it is consumed, reducing transmission losses. Ground-mounted systems, on the other hand, are set up on open land, allowing for larger installations and higher energy output. These systems are often used in utility-scale projects and can harness more sunlight, making them suitable for solar farms in suitable geographic locations.</p></p>
