<p><p>The benchtop electrolyte analyzer market includes two primary types: the electrolyte ion analyzer and the electrolyte pH analyzer. The electrolyte ion analyzer measures the concentration of specific ions, such as sodium, potassium, and chloride, in fluids, vital for various applications in clinical labs and research. In contrast, the electrolyte pH analyzer assesses the acidity or alkalinity of a solution, providing critical information for maintaining balance in biochemical processes. Both types are essential for accurate diagnostics and effective treatment monitoring.</p></p>
