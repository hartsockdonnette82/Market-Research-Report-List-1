<p><p>Bladder cancer treatment encompasses several market types, including intravesical therapy, chemotherapy, and preservation therapy. Intravesical therapy involves delivering medications directly into the bladder to target tumors, often used for superficial cancers. Chemotherapy employs systemic drugs to eradicate cancer cells, suitable for advanced stages. Preservation therapy focuses on maintaining bladder function while treating cancer, allowing for organ-sparing approaches. Each market type addresses different stages and patient needs, highlighting the diverse strategies in bladder cancer management and treatment.</p></p>
