<p><ul><li>Single-sided Edge Bander</li><li>Double-sided Edge Bander</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The edge bander market is categorized into single-sided and double-sided edge banders. Single-sided edge banders apply decorative material to one side of panels, making them ideal for applications where only one edge is visible, such as furniture production. Double-sided edge banders, on the other hand, apply edging to both sides of panels, offering a more refined finish suitable for high-quality furniture and cabinetry. Both types cater to the needs of manufacturers seeking efficiency and aesthetic appeal in woodworking.</p></p>
