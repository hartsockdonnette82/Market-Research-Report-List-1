<p><p>Human Resource Management Software (HRMS) is available in two primary market types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based HRMS is hosted on remote servers, allowing for easy access, scalability, and automatic updates, making it ideal for organizations seeking flexibility and reduced IT maintenance. In contrast, on-premises HRMS is installed locally on company servers, offering greater control over data and customization but requiring significant upfront investment and ongoing management. Both types cater to diverse organizational needs and IT infrastructures.</p></p>
