<p><ul><li>Single-spark</li><li>Multi-spark</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Sedan Ignition Coil market includes two primary types: Single-spark and Multi-spark systems. Single-spark ignition coils generate one spark per ignition cycle, delivering energy to a single spark plug, making them cost-effective and widely used in standard sedan models. Multi-spark ignition coils produce multiple sparks in rapid succession during each cycle, enhancing combustion efficiency, fuel economy, and performance, often found in high-performance or luxury sedans. Both types cater to varying consumer needs, balancing affordability and advanced functionality.</p></p>
