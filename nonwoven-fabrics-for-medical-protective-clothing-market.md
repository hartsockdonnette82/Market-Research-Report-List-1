<p><p>The market for Nonwoven Fabrics in medical protective clothing includes various types tailored for specific applications. SMS Nonwoven consists of three layers—spunbond-meltblown-spunbond—providing high barrier protection. SMMS Nonwoven adds an extra meltblown layer for enhanced filtration and softness. SMMMS Nonwoven incorporates two meltblown layers, further improving filtration efficacy and liquid repellency. Other nonwoven types may include specialized composites or coatings, catering to unique medical requirements, ensuring safety and comfort in protective apparel.</p></p>
