<p><p>The Aircraft Anti-icing and Rain Protection System market comprises two primary types: Thermal Anti-icing and Liquid Anti-icing systems. Thermal Anti-icing uses heat from engines or electrical sources to prevent ice formation on critical surfaces, enhancing safety and performance. In contrast, Liquid Anti-icing systems utilize chemical de-icing fluids sprayed on surfaces to prevent ice adhesion and improve visibility during adverse weather. Both systems are essential for maintaining aircraft safety and operational efficiency in icy or rainy conditions.</p></p>
