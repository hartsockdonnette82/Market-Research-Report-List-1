<p><strong>The Building Asphalt Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Polymer-Modified Bitumen</li><li>Bitumen Emulsion</li><li>Cutback Emulsion</li><li>Oxidized Emulsion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The building asphalt market comprises various types, each catering to specific needs. Polymer-modified bitumen enhances elasticity and durability, making it ideal for high-performance applications. Bitumen emulsion offers easy application and cold setting, suitable for surface treatments and repairs. Cutback emulsion, which contains solvents, provides quick workability but has environmental concerns. Oxidized emulsion, created through air-blowing, delivers superior stability and is commonly used for roofing and insulation, showcasing the diversity in asphalt products for construction and infrastructure projects.</p></p>
