<p><p>A Double Clutch Transmission (DCT) is an advanced automotive transmission system that uses two separate clutches for odd and even gear sets, enabling seamless and rapid gear shifts without interrupting power delivery. It combines the efficiency of a manual transmission with the convenience of an automatic, offering improved fuel efficiency and performance. DCTs are widely used in passenger cars, commercial vehicles, and high-performance sports cars due to their ability to handle high torque and provide smooth driving experiences.</p><p>The Double Clutch Transmission Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing demand for fuel-efficient and high-performance vehicles.
