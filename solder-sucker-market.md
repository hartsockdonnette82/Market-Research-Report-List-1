<p><p>The solder sucker market is divided into two main types: manual solder suckers and electric solder suckers. Manual solder suckers are simple, hand-operated devices that rely on a plunger mechanism to create suction, making them cost-effective and easy to use for basic solder removal tasks. Electric solder suckers, on the other hand, utilize a motorized pump for more efficient and powerful suction, allowing for quicker and cleaner solder removal, catering to professional and high-volume applications in electronics repair.</p></p>
