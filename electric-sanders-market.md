<p><p>The electric sanders market consists of three primary types: belt, disc, and orbital sanders. Belt sanders utilize a continuous loop of sandpaper for aggressive material removal, ideal for large, flat surfaces. Disc sanders, equipped with circular sanding pads, excel in fine sanding and shaping tasks. Orbital sanders feature a circular motion, providing a smooth finish without swirl marks, making them suitable for detailed work. Each type serves distinct applications, catering to both professional and DIY users in woodworking and renovation projects.</p></p>
