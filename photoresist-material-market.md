<p><ul><li>Positive Photoresist</li><li>Negative Photoresist</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The photoresist material market primarily comprises positive and negative photoresists, both essential in photolithography processes used for semiconductor manufacturing. Positive photoresists become soluble when exposed to light, allowing for precise pattern transfer, making them ideal for smaller features. In contrast, negative photoresists harden upon exposure, making the unexposed areas soluble, which is useful for specific applications requiring higher resolution and stability. Both types play critical roles in electronics, optoelectronics, and nanotechnology sectors, driving advancements in device fabrication.</p></p>
