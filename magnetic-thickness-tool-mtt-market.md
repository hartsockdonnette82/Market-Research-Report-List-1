<p><p>The Magnetic Thickness Tool (MTT) market is divided into two main types: Ultrasound Type and Other market. The Ultrasound Type uses high-frequency sound waves to measure material thickness, providing accurate readings without damaging the surface. This technology is widely used in industries like manufacturing and construction for inspecting coatings, pipes, and tanks. The Other market encompasses alternative non-destructive testing methods, such as electromagnetic and eddy current techniques, catering to diverse applications where specific material properties or conditions require tailored thickness measurements.</p></p>
