<p><p>The Gas Purifiers market includes two main types: Point of Use Gas Purifiers and Bulk Gas Purifiers. Point of Use Gas Purifiers are compact systems installed near the application to remove impurities from gases at the final stage, ensuring high-purity gas delivery. Bulk Gas Purifiers are larger systems used to purify gases at the source or during storage, handling high volumes for industrial or laboratory use. Both types enhance gas quality, catering to specific needs in precision applications.</p></p>
